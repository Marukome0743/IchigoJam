<h1 align="center">24LED Squash</h1>

## Code

```
10 '24LED SQUASH
20 CLV:W=24
30 @LOOP
40 LET [W*3],0,0,0
50 W=W-1:IF W<2 W=23
60 LET [W*3],0,100,0
70 FOR I=1 TO W
80 [I*3]=100
90 WS.LED 24
100 WAIT 5
110 [I*3]=0
120 NEXT
130 BEEP 24
140 FOR I=W-1 TO 1 STEP -1
150 [I*3]=100
160 WS.LED 24
170 WAIT 5
180 [I*3]=0
190 NEXT
200 IF BTN() GOTO @END
210 [0]=100
220 WA.LED 24
230 CLT
240 @RES
250 IF BTN() BEEP:[0]=0:GOTO @LOOP
260 IF TICK()<10 GOTO @RES
270 @END
280 BEEP 24,48
290 WAIT 120
300 IF !BTN() CONT
310 RUN
'''
