<h1 align="center">24LED random</h1>

## Code

```
10 '24LED RANDOM
20 FOR I=0 TO 69 STEP 3
30 LET [I],RND(50),RND(50),RND(50):IF BTN() GOTO 220
40 NEXT
50 WS.LED 24:IF BTN() GOTO 220
60 FOR I=0 TO 30 STEP 1
70 WAIT 1:IF BTN() GOTO 220
80 NEXT
100 GOTO 20

210 'TURN OFF LED
220 FOR I=0 TO 69 STEP 3
230 LET [I],0,0,0
240 NEXT
250 WS.LED 24:WAIT 30
260 IF !BTN() CONT
270 WAIT 30:RUN
```
